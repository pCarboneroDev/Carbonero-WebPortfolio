About me

Hi! My name is Pablo Carbonero, and I’m a developer with a background in both 3D Animation and Software Development. During my 3D Animation studies, I had the opportunity to work as a programmer on the indie video game Mencía: A Never Was Tale, which was later released on Steam. After that, I started a degree in Cross-Platform App Development, and as part of my internship, I contributed to building a Pokémon TCG Pocket trading app with Flutter. Although I eventually moved on, the project continues to grow and evolve. I’m passionate about mobile app development, and my main goal is to grow professionally in this field. At the same time, I’m open to opportunities in fullstack or backend development, where I can apply my problem-solving skills and keep expanding my technical knowledge. Outside of coding, I enjoy music and video games. I play the guitar and practice guttural singing. I was even part of a band that got to play live once before disbanding. Creativity and music inspire me and help me stay balanced as a developer. Feel free to check out my projects, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you’d like to collaborate!
